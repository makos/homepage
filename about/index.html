About me

My name is Mateusz (Polish for Matthew), I'm a hobbyist programmer and web developer from Poland. At the moment I'm an amateur but I'm working hard to make this my living either at a company or by freelance work. Currently I'm employed as a CAD designer / junior manager at a small private company producing steel and aluminium parts.

I'm interested in general computer programming, ranging from user scripts to automate daily tasks at my computer to games and useful applications written in compiled languages. Recently I've developed an interest in the Rust programming language. I also enjoy getting my hands dirty with old-school C programs.
